After completing the topics on network infrastructure and server deployment on AWS, the third week focused on exploring storage, database, and access management services. The core content included getting acquainted with **Amazon S3**, researching **Amazon Relational Database Service (Amazon RDS)**, and studying **AWS Identity and Access Management (IAM)** to prepare for deploying and managing applications on the AWS platform.

### Week 3 Objectives:
* Research the Amazon S3 object storage service.
* Acquire basic knowledge of Amazon Relational Database Service (Amazon RDS).
* Understand the role of Amazon RDS in managing databases on AWS.
* Understand access control mechanisms using AWS Identity and Access Management (IAM).

### Week 3 Achievements:
