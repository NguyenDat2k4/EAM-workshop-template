During the fifth week, the internship content focused on services that help deploy applications with high availability and scalability on AWS. The main focus was to study **Amazon EC2**, **Amazon EC2 Auto Scaling (ASG)**, and how to combine them with **Elastic Load Balancer (ELB)** to automatically scale the system, replace failed servers, and distribute incoming traffic across multiple EC2 Instances.

### Week 5 Objectives:
* Explore the Amazon EC2 service and its related components.
* Understand the operational mechanism of Amazon EC2 Auto Scaling.
* Research the ability to automatically replace failed EC2 Instances.
* Learn how to integrate Auto Scaling with Elastic Load Balancer to ensure high availability of the system.
