The first week of the internship primarily focused on getting acquainted with the internship environment, exploring the learning roadmap for Cloud Computing, and building a foundational knowledge of Amazon Web Services (AWS). Additionally, research was conducted on the overview of the **EAM Workspace** project to understand the objectives, scope, and development direction of the system in the subsequent phases.

### Week 1 Objectives:
* Acquire foundational knowledge and understand the ecosystem of Amazon Web Services (AWS).
* Research the learning roadmap and skill development path for Cloud Computing.
* Register for an AWS account and become familiar with basic services on the AWS Management Console.
* Research the overview of the EAM Workspace project and understand one's own role in the internship group.
