> Here, you can freely share your personal opinions about your experience participating in the First Cloud AI Journey program. This will help the FCAJ team improve any shortcomings based on the following aspects:

## Overall Evaluation

### 1. Working Environment

I evaluate the program's working environment as highly professional, friendly, and well-oriented. Throughout my participation, I could feel the seriousness in organization, clarity in each learning content, and active support from the admin team as well as other members.

What impressed me was that the program did not create a sense of heavy pressure, but instead brought an open learning space where everyone could ask questions, exchange ideas, and share difficulties comfortably. Thanks to that, I gradually became more confident in presenting my views, more active in researching new knowledge, and had more motivation to complete the assigned tasks.

This environment not only helped me acquire more professional knowledge but also helped me practice a serious working style, a sense of responsibility, and the ability to adapt to a practically-oriented training program.

### 2. Support from Team Admin

The admin team supported very quickly, clearly, and thoughtfully throughout the program. Important information, learning materials, schedules, and notifications were all provided in a timely manner, helping me easily track progress and prepare better for each activity.

When issues arose or clarification was needed, the admin team always responded clearly, creating a sense of reassurance for the students. This support made the participation process smoother, limited unnecessary difficulties, and allowed me to focus more on learning, practicing, and developing skills.

I highly appreciate the professionalism and sense of responsibility of the admin team, as this is an important factor contributing to a positive learning experience for interns.

### 3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major

The program content has a good connection with my Information Technology major. Through the program, I had the opportunity to access practical knowledge about cloud computing, AWS, AI, and project development processes. These are all important, highly applicable fields that align with current technology trends.

The point I highly value is that the program does not just stop at theory but also creates conditions for students to practice, research real services, and step-by-step get familiar with how to deploy a technology solution. Thanks to that, I better understand the connection between the knowledge learned at school and how that knowledge is applied in a real environment.

The program also helped me realize that to work well in the Information Technology industry, in addition to programming foundation and professional knowledge, learners also need self-research skills, the ability to read and understand documents, problem-solving mindset, and a continuous learning spirit.

### 4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities

The program brings me many opportunities to develop both professional knowledge and soft skills. Regarding professional knowledge, I gained access to more insights about Cloud, AWS, AI, and how to apply these technologies to real problems. Practicing and getting familiar with AWS services helped me have a more concrete view of cloud computing, rather than just understanding concepts at a theoretical level.

Regarding soft skills, I learned how to work in teams more effectively, listen to other people's opinions, divide tasks reasonably, and coordinate to complete common goals. In addition, I also practiced time management, document searching, self-learning, idea presentation, and problem-solving when facing difficulties.

A very meaningful point for me is that the program helped me form a proactive mindset. Instead of relying completely on guidance, I learned to research first, test, record errors, and proactively ask questions when needing support. This is an important skill that I believe will be very useful in my future working environment.

### 5. Culture & Team Spirit

I am very impressed with the culture of support, sharing, and growing together in the program. Members are always ready to exchange knowledge, explain unclear parts to each other, and discuss to find solutions when facing problems. This positive learning atmosphere made me feel comfortable, more confident, and not afraid to ask questions.

The team spirit in the program is clearly shown through the way everyone coordinates in activities, shares documents, supports each other during practice, and encourages each other to complete tasks. This helped me realize that in the technology field, the ability to work with others is as important as individual capacity.

For me, this is not only a learning program but also an opportunity to connect with peers who have the same orientation, same development goals, and same progressive spirit.

### 6. Internship Policies / Benefits

Although the main goal of the program is training, orientation, and skill development for interns, I still highly appreciate the practical values that the program brings. Knowledge sharing sessions, workshops, study materials, and support from the admin team have greatly assisted my learning process.

These values not only helped me improve my professional capacity but also gave me clearer orientation about my future career path. For students, getting exposure to a practical learning environment and getting familiar with widely-used technologies is a very valuable benefit.

I believe this is a good foundation for interns to prepare better before entering a professional working environment.

## Additional Questions

### What did you find most satisfying during your internship?

What I found most satisfying during my participation in the program was gaining access to practical knowledge about AWS, Cloud, and AI. This knowledge helped me better understand how modern technologies are applied in enterprises, while broadening my perspective on the Information Technology industry.

In addition to professional knowledge, I also highly appreciate the content shared about learning mindsets, career orientation, and self-development in the tech field. These things helped me realize what skills I need to prepare, where to improve, and how to orient myself in the near future.

The program has given me more motivation to continue learning, cultivating skills, and becoming more confident on my future career development path.

### What do you think the program should improve for future interns?

In my opinion, the program could organize more sharing sessions from alumni who have completed the program or are currently working in enterprises. These sharing sessions will help interns better understand the real working environment, the skills to prepare, and the common difficulties encountered when starting in the industry.

Besides, the program could increase networking activities between groups so that students have more opportunities to connect, exchange experiences, and learn from each other. Having more group activities, open discussions, or inter-group mini-projects will make the program more lively, while training teamwork and communication skills better.

Additionally, if possible, the program should supplement practice sessions based on real-world situations, in which students are presented with a specific problem and step-by-step propose, build, or present a solution. This will help students understand more deeply how to apply knowledge in practice.

### If recommending to a friend, would you suggest they participate in the program? Why or why not?

Yes. I will recommend this program to my friends, especially those studying Information Technology or interested in Cloud, AWS, and AI. In my opinion, this is a very suitable program for students because it not only provides professional knowledge but also creates opportunities for learners to practice skills, expand their mindset, and get familiar with a professional learning environment.

The reason I advise them to participate is because the program brings practical experience and helps students better understand current industry requirements. Through the program, learners can better identify their career path, recognize their strengths and areas for improvement, thereby preparing themselves better before entering the workforce after graduation.

This is a valuable opportunity for those who want to learn seriously, proactively develop themselves, and get early exposure to highly applicable technologies.

## Suggestions & Expectations

### Do you have any suggestions to improve the internship experience?

I hope the program can add more practice sessions based on real-world situations, where students get familiar with problems close to the corporate environment. For example, the program can give a specific requirement related to AWS, Cloud, or AI, and then let students analyze the problem, propose a solution, and present the results. This way of learning will help students understand deeper, remember longer, and practice applying knowledge better.

In addition, I hope there will be more specialized workshops on AWS and AI, especially highly applicable topics such as deploying services on cloud, building solutions using AI, basic security on AWS, or tech project development processes. These contents will help students have more practical perspectives and prepare better for their future work.

I also hope the program organizes more connection activities between students, such as experience sharing sessions, group discussions, networking activities, or group mini-projects. This will help students have more opportunities to exchange, learn from each other, and build better team spirit.

### Would you like to continue this program in the future?

Yes. If the program continues to organize advanced courses, activities, or in-depth training, I really hope to have the opportunity to participate. After the experience, I found that the program brings many practical values, not only in terms of knowledge but also in terms of learning mindset, work skills, and career orientation.

I hope in the future to continue learning more about AWS, Cloud, AI, as well as new technologies being applied in practice. Participating in programs like this will help me maintain a learning spirit, update knowledge continuously, and develop myself more sustainably.

### Other Comments

I would like to express my sincere thanks to the entire FCAJ team and the admin team who have always supported, guided, and accompanied me dedicatedly during my participation in the program. The thoughtfulness in organization, the active supporting spirit, and the open learning environment have created a very memorable experience.

For me, the program not only helped me gain more knowledge about AWS, Cloud, and AI, but also helped me grow in my mindset, learning methods, and working style. I learned that to develop in the tech field, each person needs to constantly update knowledge, proactively research, persevere when facing difficulties, and know how to coordinate with others to achieve better results.

Hope the program will continue to develop, expand more practical contents, and bring many quality learning opportunities to students in subsequent cohorts. I believe that with serious investment and dedicated support from the organizing team, the program will create more and more meaningful values for the technology-loving student community.
