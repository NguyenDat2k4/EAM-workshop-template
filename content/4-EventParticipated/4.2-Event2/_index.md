# REPORT ON PARTICIPATING IN AWS FIRST CLOUD JOURNEY COMMUNITY DAY EVENT

## 1. Event Information

* **Event Name:** AWS First Cloud Journey Community Day
* **Time:** June 6, 2026
* **Location:** 26th Floor, Bitexco Financial Tower
* **Role:** Attendee of the First Cloud Journey program
* **Format:** In-person participation

---

## 2. Participation Objective

I participated in this event to expand my practical knowledge of Cloud Computing, DevOps, security, artificial intelligence, and modern technologies on the AWS platform. Additionally, this was an opportunity to learn from the experiences of industry experts, obtain career guidance, and improve my technical skills to support my academic studies and internship.

---

## 3. Main Content

The event featured multiple technical sessions on AWS and modern technologies, including:

* **Docker and Containerization:** An introduction to container technology, the process of packaging and deploying applications, and Docker's role in DevOps and Cloud environments.
* **Machine Learning-based NIDS on AWS:** An overview of integrating AWS WAF with Machine Learning to detect and prevent cyberattacks more effectively.
* **The Journey from IT Helpdesk to Senior Sysadmin:** Sharing career development experiences, key skills in Linux, Networking, Cloud, and system administration.
* **AWS WebSockets for Real-time Applications:** Presenting a serverless architecture using API Gateway, Lambda, and DynamoDB to build real-time applications.
* **Teamwork Skills:** Sharing principles of communication, coordination, and using project management tools such as Trello, Slack, and Google Workspace.
* **GraphRAG with Amazon Bedrock and Neptune:** Introducing AI models combined with graph database systems to enhance search and reasoning capabilities in AI systems.

---

## 4. Key Takeaways

Following the event, I gained a better understanding of Docker's role, serverless architectures, security on the AWS platform, and the trends of applying AI in enterprises. Furthermore, I recognized the importance of building foundational knowledge in operating systems, computer networking, security, and teamwork skills to thrive in the Cloud and DevOps fields.

---

## 5. Personal Contribution

Throughout the program, I actively followed the presentations, took notes on key topics, and cross-referenced them with the knowledge gained during my internship at AWS. Based on this, I identified areas needing further study and developed a plan to improve my skills in Docker, Serverless, Cloud Security, and AI.

---

## 6. Conclusion

The AWS First Cloud Journey Community Day event provided valuable practical knowledge and new perspectives on modern technologies on the AWS platform. The event helped me reinforce my professional knowledge, clarify my career path, and inspired me to continue learning and researching to meet the requirements of the Information Technology industry in the future.
