Sharing and Feedback

Here, you can freely share your personal opinions about your experience participating in the First Cloud AI Journey program. This will help the FCAJ team improve any shortcomings based on the following aspects:

Overall Evaluation

1. Working Environment

 I evaluate the working environment of the program as very professional, friendly, and motivating. Throughout my participation, I always received support from mentors and team members whenever I encountered difficulties. The open working atmosphere helped me confidently express my ideas and take more initiative in my studies.

2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin

 Mentors always provided dedicated guidance, answered my questions, and offered constructive feedback to help me improve my knowledge and skills. The admin team was also prompt in providing information, materials, and resolving issues that arose, ensuring my participation in the program went smoothly.

3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major

 The program content aligned well with my Information Technology major. I had the opportunity to learn about cloud computing, AI, and real-world project development processes, applying what I learned to practical execution while acquiring new insights.

4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities

 The program offered numerous opportunities to develop both my professional knowledge and soft skills. I learned how to work effectively in a team, manage time, conduct research, solve problems, and access AWS services through hands-on practical exercises.

5. Culture & Team Spirit

 I was highly impressed by the supportive and collaborative spirit among the program participants. Everyone was always willing to share knowledge and discuss solutions to difficulties, creating a positive learning environment and making me feel comfortable throughout my participation.

6. Internship Policies / Benefits

 Although the main goal of the program is training and skill development, I highly value the knowledge-sharing sessions, workshops, and study materials provided. These are practical values that helped me enhance my professional capabilities.

Additional Questions

  • What did you find most satisfying during your internship?

    • What I found most satisfying was learning from highly experienced mentors and gaining exposure to real-world knowledge of AWS, Cloud, and AI. These sharing sessions helped clarify my career path and improve my technical skills.

  • What do you think the program should improve for future interns?

    • In my opinion, the program could organize more sharing sessions with alumni or seniors working in tech companies. Additionally, increasing networking activities between teams would give everyone more opportunities to learn and connect.

  • If recommending to a friend, would you suggest they participate in the program? Why or why not?

    • Yes. I would recommend it because this program helps students access real-world knowledge of Cloud and AI, learning from experienced mentors in a professional environment. This is an excellent opportunity to prepare before entering the professional workforce after graduation.

Suggestions & Expectations

  • Do you have any suggestions to improve the internship experience?

    • I hope the program will offer more practice sessions based on real-world scenarios, specialized workshops on AWS and AI, and more networking activities among participants to increase opportunities for experience exchange and teamwork.

  • Would you like to continue this program in the future?

    • Yes. If the program continues to organize advanced courses or activities, I would love the opportunity to participate to update my knowledge and develop my professional skills.

  • Any other comments (free sharing):

    • I would like to express my gratitude to the entire FCAJ team, the mentors, and the admin team who have always supported me dedicatedly during my participation in the program. This has been a very memorable experience that helped me grow in knowledge, skills, and working mindset. I hope the program will continue to develop and bring high-quality learning opportunities to students in future cohorts.
